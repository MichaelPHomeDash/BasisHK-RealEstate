## Selected Approach: Neo-Urban Noir

**Reasoning**: Hong Kong is a high-energy, vertical city defined by its skyline and speed. Real estate agents here are competitive and need to feel like they are on the cutting edge. The "Neo-Urban Noir" style captures the essence of the city's nightscapeâ€”where deals are made and social media scrolling happens. It differentiates PropelHK from traditional, stuffy agencies by positioning it as a tech-forward, "insider" partner that unlocks the hidden potential of the market.

**Design Philosophy**: "Illuminating the Market." We use light (neon accents) against the dark (market noise) to guide agents to their leads.

**Implementation Notes**:
- **Backgrounds**: Dark gradients (slate to black) with subtle noise texture.
- **Accents**: Electric Blue and Magenta gradients for buttons and highlights.
- **Images**: High-contrast, night-time city shots, or stylized device mockups showing social feeds glowing in the dark.
- **Font**: Space Grotesk for headers to give that "future-tech" vibe.
